We are helping product teams in design research

Curated collection of users flows

When the product team works on new products or features they spent a lot of time researching similar products in the market. Usually, this process involves steps like identifying similar products, signing up and going through the products, making notes, taking screenshots, etc.

We are trying to save hours of time spent on such research activities. We have created a library of user flows of successful products in the world. product teams can come to UiFLO and refer 100s of User flows recording.
